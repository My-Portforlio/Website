My Approach

Strategy & Growth Approach

"Growth isn't luck — it's strategy, consistency, and knowing exactly who you're talking to."

As a professional social media manager, I focus on understanding the business first — its audience, positioning, and goals. I use data and performance insights to identify exactly who the target audience is and how they interact online.

From there, I build a strategy based on what's proven to work — using metrics, trends, and behaviour patterns to guide content and growth. Whether the goal is attracting a business audience, driving purchases, or increasing visibility, every decision is intentional and backed by analysis.

The goal is simple: reach the right people, with the right content, at the right time — and turn that attention into real growth.
