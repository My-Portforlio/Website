Meet the Founder & CEO

David Ayogu

"Helping businesses move from scattered efforts to structured, scalable, high-performing systems."

Growthive Systems was founded by David Ayogu, a business strategist and creative operator with a background in industrial/organisational psychology focused on helping small and medium enterprises achieve structured, sustainable growth. His work sits at the intersection of creative innovation and growth-driven strategy, where ideas are transformed into structured systems that enable businesses to scale with clarity and consistency. With experience across branding, digital media, and organizational development, he builds businesses that are not only visible, but also designed to perform, adapt, and expand in evolving markets. His approach integrates digital presence, strategic planning, and internal structure into a unified framework turning fragmented efforts into measurable progress and long-term value. Driven by discipline, purpose, and a commitment to excellence, David is focused on creating solutions that deliver both immediate impact and sustainable growth.

Beyond business, he is committed to building impact through his foundation, supporting growth, learning, and opportunities within communities.
